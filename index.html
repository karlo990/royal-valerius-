Our Story

A Legacy of Scientific Excellence

Located in Nyika, Masvingo, Zimbabwe, Royal Valerius Science Academy has proudly delivered specialist tuition across 8 successful streams. We offer Sciences and Technical/Vocational subjects, with personalised private lessons and fully equipped boarding facilities, giving every student the focused environment they need to excel.

Our experienced educators are dedicated to one mission — transforming students into top performers. We combine rigorous academic standards with a nurturing environment that draws the very best from each learner.
